FAQ

Common questions

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can start with Starter and upgrade to Professional at any time — you'll only pay the difference.

What's included in "support"?

Support covers bug fixes, minor content updates, and answering questions about your site during the support period.

Do you offer monthly payment plans?

Yes — for Professional and Enterprise plans we offer split payments. Contact us to set up an arrangement that works for you.

How long does a project take?

Starter projects typically take 1–2 weeks. Professional projects take 3–6 weeks. Enterprise timelines vary by scope.

Who owns the code when it's done?

You do. Once the final payment is made, you receive full ownership of all code and design assets.

What if I need something not listed?

Just reach out. We handle custom projects all the time — we'll put together a scoped quote for exactly what you need.

Ready to get started?
